# Excecutive-Power-Bi-Report

**Overview**

This project presents an executive Power BI analysis of a European pharmacy distribution network, covering 8 countries and 31 regions between July 2024 and June 2025. The report focuses on evaluating sales performance, profitability, and key business drivers to support data-driven decision-making.

**Objectives**

•Analyze revenue and margin distribution across regions

•Evaluate performance by store type and size

•Identify top-performing product categories

•Assess promotion effectiveness

•Highlight opportunities for growth and optimization

**Dataset**

The dataset contains daily pharmacy-level transactions structured into:

•Fact Sales Table

•Product Table

•Region Table

•Date Table

**Data Preparation**

•Created key DAX measures: Revenue, Cost, Margin, Margin %

•Built hierarchical structure: Country → Region → Pharmacy

•Developed time-based analysis (Month, Quarter, Year)

•Validated data consistency and relationships

**Key Insights**

•Total Revenue: €8.63M | Profit: €2.42M | Margin: 28%

•Germany leads in both revenue and profitability

•Wellness category shows higher profit efficiency than OTC

•Urban stores generate the highest revenue (47%)

•Medium-sized stores contribute the most profit (44%)

•Promotions account for only ~10% of total revenue

**Recommendations**

•Expand high-margin Wellness products

•Optimize OTC pricing and cost structure

•Replicate efficiency of medium-sized stores

•Improve performance in low-revenue regions

•Re-evaluate promotion strategy for better ROI

**Limitations**

•No customer-level data

•No inventory or operational cost breakdown

•Limited promotion details

•No competitor analysis

*Tools Used*

•Power BI

•DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)

**Conclusion**

The analysis reveals strong overall business performance, with clear opportunities in category optimization, store efficiency, and regional growth. Strategic focus on high-margin products and operational improvements can drive future scalability.
